**Tool Knowledge**

The recent_chats tool does not surface conversations stored inside Claude Projects — it only retrieves conversations from the main, non-Project chat history. When the person's conversation history is organized into Project folders, those threads are invisible to this tool. There is currently no available tool to search within Projects from outside them; the person must navigate directly to the relevant Project on Claude.ai to continue those conversations.
